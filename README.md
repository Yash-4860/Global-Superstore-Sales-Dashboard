# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, profit, and order performance for the Global Superstore dataset across regions, categories, segments, and years.

<img width="1372" height="772" alt="Dashboard Preview" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/35e3e7c6-cb21-487b-8e32-5761cf5ba6e3" />


## Overview

This dashboard provides an interactive view of global sales performance, allowing users to filter by **Region**, **Category**, and **Year** to explore trends and KPIs in real time.

## Key Metrics (KPI Cards)

- **Total Sales:** 13M
- **Total Orders:** 25K
- **Total Profit:** 1M
- **Total Quantity:** 178K

## Visuals Included

- **Sales Trend** — Monthly sales trend line from 2011–2014, highlighting overall growth over time.
- **Profit by Category** — Horizontal bar chart comparing profit across Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture.
- **Total Sales by Region** — Horizontal bar chart ranking regions (Central, South, North, Oceania, Southeast Asia, North Asia, EMEA, Africa) by total sales.
- **Sales by Segment** — Donut chart breaking down sales share across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.

## Filters / Slicers

- **Region:** Africa, Canada, Caribbean, Central, Central Asia, East, EMEA, North, North Asia, Oceania, South, Southeast Asia, West
- **Category:** Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology
- **Year:** 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014

## Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop** — data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization
- **Global Superstore dataset** — sales transactions dataset (region, category, segment, order, and profit level detail)

## How to Use

1. Download `Global_Superstore_Sales_Dashboard.pbix`
2. Open it in [Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/desktop/) (free)
3. Use the slicers on the left to filter by Region, Category, or Year
4. Hover over charts for detailed tooltips
